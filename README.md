# 🎧 ya-namp - Enjoy Yandex Music with Winamp style

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-ya--namp-blue)](https://github.com/CodeMonkeyw/ya-namp)

ya-namp brings the look and feel of the classic Winamp music player to your Yandex Music library. This application lets you listen to your tracks through a nostalgic, customizable interface. Because it runs inside your web browser, you do not need to install complex software on your hard drive. 

## 🚀 How to start
You access this player directly through your internet browser without any setup. 

1. Visit the [ya-namp repository page](https://github.com/CodeMonkeyw/ya-namp).
2. Click the link provided to launch the player.
3. Log in with your Yandex account credentials.
4. Select your favorite playlists or albums to begin playback.

## 🛠️ System Requirements
This player works on most modern computers. 

*   **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   **Web Browser:** Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Mozilla Firefox.
*   **Internet Connection:** A stable connection allows for smooth streaming of high-quality audio files. 
*   **Memory:** At least 4GB of RAM ensures the browser runs the interface without lag.

## ✨ Main Features
The player mimics the behavior of the original Winamp software while connecting to your modern Yandex Music library.

*   **Classic Visuals:** The interface centers on the recognizable Winamp skin. You see familiar sliders and frequency displays as your music plays.
*   **Library Sync:** The program connects directly to your Yandex Music account. It fetches your existing playlists and liked songs automatically.
*   **Browser-Based:** You run this tool directly in your browser. This reduces the risk of system errors and saves space on your computer.
*   **Keyboard Controls:** Use your media keys or standard keyboard shortcuts to pause, play, or skip tracks while you work in other windows.
*   **Audio Quality:** The player supports the stream quality settings established in your Yandex Music profile. 

## ⚙️ Usage Tips
Follow these steps to manage your audio experience:

*   **Authenticating:** When you first load the page, the application asks for permission to access your Yandex Music data. Accept these prompts to view your music collection inside the player.
*   **Customizing Skins:** While the default look mirrors the iconic Winamp design, you can often toggle between light and dark versions if the settings menu allows it.
*   **Managing Playlists:** Drag and drop tracks within the playlist editor to rearrange your current queue. Changes happen in real-time.
*   **Troubleshooting:** If the player stops responding, refresh your browser tab. Since the software runs in the browser, a page reload clears common errors. 

## 📘 Frequently Asked Questions
**Does this player install files on my PC?**
No. This tool runs entirely in your web browser. It does not modify your system files or install background processes.

**Is my music data safe?**
The application uses official pathways to connect to Yandex Music. It does not store your password on your computer.

**Can I use this offline?**
No. This player requires an active internet connection to stream audio files from Yandex servers. 

**Does it support local files?**
Currently, the program focuses on streaming from the Yandex service. It does not play files stored locally on your hard drive.

This software serves as a bridge between a contemporary music service and a legacy design. It uses web technologies to recreate the experience of 90s media players.
